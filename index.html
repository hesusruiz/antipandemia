Post Pandemic Credentials

Getting back to a normal functioning of our societies and economies and to sustainable growth.

Verifiable Credentials and Blockchain

Getting back to normal

But not only for essential workers.

We have to prove facts about ourselves

Results of covid tests.

To go back to work, to enter in crowded spaces or to travel.

And we have to say things about others or about ourselves.

As an employer

That our employees have to go somewhere, or that our business complies with health procedures, or many others to re-gain confidence of customers.

As an individual

eg. that our family is free from the virus.

We need two types of certificates

Official certificates

Issued by official institutions.

Like results of Covid tests.

Self-declarations

"I responsably declare that ..."

When official certificates are not feasible.

We have to avoid paper certificates

Digital credentials

In a standard format.

Digital wallets

For storing digital credentials.

Standards, Standards, Standards

Storing credentials

In the user's wallets.

Verifying credentials

By the employers.

Issuing credentials

By the medical centers.

Interoperate with credential issuers and verifiers
